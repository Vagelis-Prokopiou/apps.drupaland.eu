PeribiblicumApp

PeribiblicumApp is an Android application (created with PhoneGap and AngularJS), that provides you instant access to all the blog posts of peribiblicum.wordpress.com.

Check out every new post quickly and easily!

It is available through Google Play at the following url:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.github.Vaggos.phonegap

Enjoy!
